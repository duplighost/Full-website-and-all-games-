You Hear a Silly Scream from your Freezer

A short first-person ethical incident simulator about a kitten, a freezer, and a banana-shaped category error with lungs it probably should not have.

Phone: put your left thumb anywhere on the left side to move and your right thumb anywhere on the right side to look. Keyboard: WASD + mouse + E.
